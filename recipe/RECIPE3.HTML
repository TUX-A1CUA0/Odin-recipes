SCOTTISH EGG

Description

A encase egg with meat and crumbs

ingredients

- 1 pound sausage meat
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard powder
- 4 large eggs (soft or hard-boiled)
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup breadcrumbs
- 1/2 teaspoon paprika (optional)
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder (optional)
- 1 additional egg (beaten, for coating)
- Oil for frying

STEPS

1. **Season the sausage meat with salt, pepper, and mustard powder.**
2. **Divide the sausage meat into 4 equal portions.**
3. **Flatten each portion into a thin patty.**
4. **Wrap each boiled egg with the sausage meat, ensuring the egg is completely covered.**
5. **Coat each sausage-wrapped egg in flour.**
6. **Dip each floured egg into the beaten egg.**
7. **Roll each egg in breadcrumbs, pressing gently to adhere.**
8. **Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).**
9. **Carefully lower each coated egg into the hot oil.**
10. **Fry for 4-5 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden brown and crispy.**
11. **Remove the eggs from the oil and drain on paper towels.**
12. **Let the Scotch eggs cool slightly before serving.**
