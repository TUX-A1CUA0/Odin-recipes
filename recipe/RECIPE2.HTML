SINIGANG

Description

DETECT YOUR PALLETE WITH PILIPINO DELICACY,A SOUR SOUP WITH PORK IN IT BREV

ingredients 

- Pork (belly or ribs)
- Water
- Tamarind soup mix (Sinigang mix)
- Tomatoes
- Onion
- Radish
- Eggplant
- Long green beans (sitaw)
- Water spinach (kangkong) or bok choy

(Optional)
- Fish sauce
- Green chili peppers
- Salt and pepper

STEPS

1. **Cut the pork into bite-sized pieces.**
2. **Bring 6 cups of water to a boil in a large pot.**
3. **Add the pork to the boiling water and simmer for 30-40 minutes, skimming off any scum.**
4. **Add the quartered tomatoes and onion to the pot and cook for 5 minutes.**
5. **Add the sliced radish and eggplant, and cook for another 5 minutes.**
6. **Add the long green beans and cook for 3-4 minutes.**
7. **Stir in the tamarind soup mix until fully dissolved.**
8. **Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste.**
9. **Add green chili peppers if desired for heat.**
10. **Stir in the water spinach or bok choy and cook for 2-3 minutes until wilted.**
11. **Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.**
12. **Serve hot with steamed rice.**
