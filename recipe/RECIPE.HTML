ingreedients

        Certainly! Here's a simplified list of ingredients:

- Spaghetti pasta
- Olive oil
- Garlic
- Onion
- Tomato sauce
- Salt
- Pepper

(Optional)
- Parmesan cheese
- Fresh basil or parsley
- Ground beef or sausage

STEPS

        Sure! Here's a simplified step-by-step guide:

    Step 1: Cook the Pasta
    1. Boil water in a large pot.
    2. Add spaghetti and cook for 8-10 minutes.
    3. Drain the pasta.

    Step 2: Make the Sauce
    1. Heat olive oil in a pan.
    2. Cook minced garlic and chopped onion until soft.
    3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.

    Step 3: Combine and Serve
    1. Mix the cooked spaghetti with the sauce.
    2. Serve with optional Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs.

    Enjoy!
